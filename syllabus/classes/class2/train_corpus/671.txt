(Alcala, 1561) 1562 St Teresa, a Spanish reformer, includes chess in her writings. 1570 Gianutto della Mantia, Horatio born in Italy. Author of Italian chess book. 1572 Ruy Lopez defeats several eminent players in Rome. 1574 Boi and Leonardo beat Lopez & Ceron in presence of Phillip II. 1575 Leonardo beats Ruy Lopez in Madrid. 1575 After the Plague of Cremona, all games except chess were banned. 1576 Boi taken prisoner, but wins his freedom playing chess. 1580 Catherine de Medici of France is a keen chess player.
