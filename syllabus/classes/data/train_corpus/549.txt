The self of the individual lies hidden in the innermost layers of existence (kosas). Thus kaoshikii is a blossoming of the microcosm (unit being) in its attempt to attain a link with the Macrocosm (Cosmic Consciousness). This attempt is normally referred to as mysticism. -- The Benefits Exercises all the glands and limbs from head to toes. Increases longevity. Makes for easy delivery at childbirth. The spine will become flexible. Arthritis of the spine, neck, waist and other joints will be removed.
